Elementalist Mage

Masters of elemental runes, Huppermages combine the elements of Fire, Air, Water and Earth to create breathtakingly powerful spells. Driven by their thirst for knowledge and the importance of their mission, they tirelessly study ways to re-establish the Krosmic Balance, guarantor of stability in the World of Twelve.

roles Mobility
Versatility: Huppermages are multi-talented in combat, able to hit in all elements using other classes' magic, and inflicting considerable damage thanks to their specialty spells.
Support
By accumulating enough energy during combat, the Huppermages release all their power on one enemy or on a group.
Control
The majority of Huppermage spells have side effects, including hindrance and positioning effects. They can also place devices on the battlefield to restrict their opponents' positioning.

It's the "Destructive Arrow" spell, borrowed from the Cra class. The Huppermage brandishes a majestic light arrow, which they loose to strike their target with full force. The spell has additional effects depending on the last rune generated.
